About Ventrai

A place where mentees find mentors and learn their most favored skill.

Our Mission

Ventrai's mission is to create a space where every learner feels supported. This means connecting with mentors who understand what you're going through, and a community where you can ask questions, get advice, and grow together without pressure.

At the core, We also believe every mentor and mentee feels valued and every story matters. Our platform is built to make it easy for you to share your real-world experiences and help the next generation move forward with confidence.

How Ventrai Works

Create an Account, Go to the sign up page and create an account either using google or default by email.

Find a Mentor, Go to the mentors' page and view all mentors and when they are available by clicking on them.

Chat away and Learn!, Go to your chat page, click on the designated chat, and ask your mentor questions while learning new things!

The Impact of Social Media

Ventrai was made because of how social media posts things that are not appropriate for the young people coming online. Some highlights of Ventrai:

  • Blocking bad words sent through chats.
  • Makes sure users verify their identity through email before they are allowed in.
  • Has an Availability page where mentors and mentees can set their available times to avoid confusions.
